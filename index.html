Frequently Asked Questions

Are these scripts safe to use?

All scripts in our database are scanned for malicious code before being approved. However, using any exploit or script in Roblox is against the Terms of Service and could result in your account being banned. Use at your own risk.

Why isn't my script working?

There could be several reasons: the game might have been updated, the script might be patched, or your executor might not be injecting properly. Try checking our Discord for updates or searching for an alternative script.

How often are scripts updated?

Our team and community members work daily to update scripts as games patch them. Popular game scripts are usually updated within 24-48 hours of being patched.

Can I request a script?

Yes! You can submit script requests through our Discord server or the contact form below. Please include the game name and desired features.

How do I copy the scripts?

Simply click the "Copy" button on any script card, then paste it into your executor's script executor. Some executors may require you to press "Execute" or "Inject" after pasting.
